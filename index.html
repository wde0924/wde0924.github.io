Research Theme:

The overarching goal is to investigate human impacts on carbon, pollution, water, and energy fluxes using observations as key constraints! In our rapidly changing climate, our generation faces key challenges related to the carbon and water/energy cycles: mitigating anthropogenic and pyrogenic emissions while enhancing carbon sequestration and ensuring food security while coping with water scarcity.
